# Celebrate 70 — Design Concepts

<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement:** Scottish Romanticism meets Modern Cartography
**Core Principles:** Rugged warmth, navigational clarity, celebratory intimacy, offline-first legibility
**Color Philosophy:** Deep Caledonian forest green (#1a3a2a) as the primary canvas, with warm amber/gold (#c9922a) as the celebratory accent evoking whisky and heather honey. Soft cream (#f5f0e8) for card surfaces, slate grey for secondary text. The palette feels like a hand-drawn Ordnance Survey map with a warm evening fire.
**Layout Paradigm:** Vertical timeline spine running down the left edge of the screen — each day "hangs" off the spine like a waypoint on a trail map. Bottom navigation bar with 4 tabs: Today, Journey, Explore, Share.
**Signature Elements:** Thin topographic line-art watermark behind hero sections; hand-drawn-style location pin icons; subtle tartan texture on the header bar.
**Interaction Philosophy:** Swipe between days on the timeline; tap a location pin to expand its detail card; long-press a ferry booking to reveal the QR code.
**Animation:** Timeline entries slide in from the right on first load; the "current" event pulses gently; map pins drop in with a spring bounce.
**Typography System:** Display: "Playfair Display" (serif, bold) for headings and celebration moments. Body: "Source Sans 3" (clean, legible at small sizes). Monospace: "JetBrains Mono" for booking references and times.
</idea>
